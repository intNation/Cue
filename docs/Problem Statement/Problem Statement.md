# Problem Statement
Currently, Students struggle to initiate studing, even when what needs to be done is understood

Students strugle to initiate study sessions due to various reasons including mental load, schedule, fatigue, environmental conditions.
As a result, they delay studying leading to procastination and fraustration.

Current systems focuses on study streaks, motivation, planning but the lack the ability to provide students with awareness
to realize patterns that influence to the difficulty in starting study sessions.

As a result, student lack the insights to understand the underying reasons why starting may feel harder on some days than others,
Making it difficult to make informed Decisions to udjust their routines
