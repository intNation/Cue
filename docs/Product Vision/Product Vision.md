# Cue - Your Partenr at helping you realise failure patterns

## What is Cue?
Cue is a mobile application that answer two questions for students: What and why.

***The what*** 
as a student, i often found myself contemplating studying. often times i would delay starting to study due to various reasons.
one common reason i was always overwhelmed to start studying was due to the heavy workload i had to complete, including assignments,
practicals, attending classes, preparing a meal, attending group meetings. but this was the common reason, not the only reason. 

So as a result i would always find myself procastinating indefinitely. 
this led me to be always a friend to fraustration and guilt.

**what** kept me from starting to study? the answer is simple: **OVERWHELM**

***The why***
From all the things that kept me from actually initiating my study sessions, none did i know why those things kept me from actually starting.
I have watched a ton of youtube tutorials, read blogs, search on google, used AI, to actually learn how to study **"*fast and effectively"***, but after all that effort, i barely applied any of the things i read or watched.
    I did this because it gave me a burst of motivation to actually start studying but that motivation did not persist over days or weeks.

So, **Why** was i  **Overwhelmed** in the first place?
I have now completed undergraduate degree of Computer Science and on this day  i do not know what led to the overwhelm in the first place.
This is what **CUE** is hoping to achieve: Helping you realise your exact patterns that lead to your ability to start studying.


### Core Product Vision
Cue is a Mobile Application That Help student realize why starting study sessions feels more difficult than some other days.

Instead of offering advice, motivation - cue focuses on awareness  - helping students realise the patterns that influence
their ability to start studying 

## What CUE is not.
Cue is NOT:
- A habit Tracker
- streaks based productivity app
- A motivational or advisor application.

Cue does not tell students what to do, It simply tell them what is happening

## What is the goal of CUE? 
According to Google, the formal definition of cue is: "a signal for action.". That's all what cue is about: Giving signals.
It shall give students signals, not to start studying, but it should give them indication to **Why** they sometimes feel like it is hard to start studying.

## How will CUE work? 
Cue will **detect failure patterns** for initiating study sessions using contextual awareness.
Thing cue will silently collect - without neglecting user's privacy are:

1. Phone usage prior to studying
2. Wheather conditions
3. Location(Optional)
4. Sleep
5. Movement(Steps/Distance Travelled)
6. Internet Connectivity.

The user's task is to record regular check-ins through minimal effort binary taps (Yes/No), The application shall do the rest.
